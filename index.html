Welcome to Control and Automation Group

Department of Electrical Engineering
Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi

The Control and Automation Group has been an integral part of the department of Electrical Engineering. Our mission is to promote cutting-edge research and innovation in the field of Control Engineering.

Research Areas

The Group includes eminent faculty members with diverse research interest, spanning the following thrust areas:

Nonlinear and Robust Control

Robotics and Embedded Control

Discrete Time Systems and Variable Structure Control

Reinforcement Learning and Adaptive Control

Systems Biology, Bio-molecular Circuit Design

Model Order Reduction

Optimal Control, Switched and Hybrid Systems

Descriptor Systems, Graph Theoretic Control

Multi-agent Systems
