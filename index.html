For Prospective Students

To join the Control and Automation Group at IIT Delhi, you typically need to pursue a postgraduate program, such as an M.Tech or Ph.D., in relevant disciplines like Electrical Engineering or Instrumentation. Admission is primarily through the Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) for M.Tech, with a strong emphasis on your GATE score, academic performance, and research interests. For Ph.D. programs, a good academic record, along with a strong research proposal aligned with the group's focus areas, is crucial. It's also beneficial to familiarize yourself with the faculty's research work and reach out to potential supervisors. Participation in relevant internships, projects, and research during your undergraduate studies can strengthen your application.
